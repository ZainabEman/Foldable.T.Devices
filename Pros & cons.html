Pros & Cons

Advantages

  1. Larger display in less space
  2. Have better power and greater durability.
  3. Have rear cameras as your selfie camera thanks to the cover display
  4. Have large tablet size screen that can be folded to fit in your pocket.
  5. Greater space for hardware

Disdvantages

  1. These devices are quite expensive
  2. Screens are still fragile
  3. Go through an irreversibly deforming process each time a material folds or unfolds
  4. Only for easy learners.
  5. Are susceptible to errors easily
  6. Have more weight than normal devices.
  7. Screens are easily exposed to scratches
